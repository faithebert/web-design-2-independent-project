ABOUT US

The Arnprior & District Humane Society is a no-kill shelter that takes in animals from Arnprior and the surrounding area. We are a non-profit organization supported through fundraising events, sponsor assistance, and kind donations. We are dedicated to finding loving, forever homes for abused, abandoned and neglected animals. It is your financial support that allows us to continue rescuing, housing, and caring for these wonderful animals that would otherwise be homeless. We are affiliated with the Ontario Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (OSPCA).
